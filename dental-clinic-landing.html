Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes — we work with most major dental insurance providers including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and United Healthcare. Our front desk team will verify your benefits before your visit so there are no surprises.

How long does a routine check-up take?

A standard exam and professional cleaning typically takes 45–60 minutes. If we haven't seen you before, allow an extra 15 minutes for paperwork and a more thorough initial assessment.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Absolutely. Our professional whitening treatments are clinically tested and completely safe when administered by a dentist. We offer in-office Zoom whitening and custom take-home trays. Both can lighten teeth by several shades with minimal sensitivity.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call us immediately at +1 (415) 882-7100. We reserve same-day slots for dental emergencies. For a knocked-out tooth, keep it moist (milk or saliva) and get to us within an hour for the best chance of saving it.

At what age should my child first see a dentist?

The American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry recommends a child's first dental visit by age 1 or within 6 months of their first tooth erupting. Early visits help establish good habits and let us catch potential issues early.

Do you offer flexible payment plans?

Yes — we partner with CareCredit and Sunbit to offer 0% interest financing for up to 18 months on qualifying treatments. We also accept all major credit cards, cash, and HSA/FSA accounts.
